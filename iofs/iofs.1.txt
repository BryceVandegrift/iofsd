iofs (1)
========

Name
----
iofs - Manage KnightOS filesystems over a silverlink cable.

Synopsis
--------
'iofs' _command_ [args...]

Commands
--------

*ls* <path>::
	Lists the contents of _path_.

Authors
-------

Maintained by Drew DeVault <sir@cmpwn.com>, who is assisted by other open
source contributors. For more information about kiofs development, see
<https://github.com/KnightOS/kiofs>.
